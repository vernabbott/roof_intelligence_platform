# TPO - Roof Damage

## Description

Standing water that remains on the roof after rainfall.

## Why it Matters

Can accelerate membrane deterioration.

May indicate poor drainage.

May lead to seam failure.

## Visual Characteristics

- Dark reflective areas
- Circular water accumulation
- Water around drains
- Water near rooftop equipment
- Staining around ponding area

## AI Confidence Indicators

High Confidence

- Clearly visible water
- Shadow reflections
- Multiple puddles

Medium Confidence

- Dark staining
- Sediment rings

Low Confidence

- Single dark area
- Cloud shadows

## Severity

Critical
